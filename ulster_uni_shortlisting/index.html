  • Ulster University
    Employee Proposition


    Ulster University is more than just a place to work, it's a place to enhance your lifestyle. We offer a unique and enriching working environment where you can develop your talents, contribute to meaningful work, and achieve your career goals. On top of all of that we offer you many options to help you with your life outside of work.

Financial

  • Pay

    We ensure timely pay disbursal, three days before month-end, facilitating effective financial planning for our employees

  • Pensions

    Sponsored pension scheme for long-term financial security and stability, an essential part of the benefits package for all Ulster University staff

  • Salary Sacrifice

    Maximize your benefits with our salary sacrifice schemes, including pensions, Bike to Work, and childcare support, providing tax advantages and cost savings

  • Wage Stream

    Build financial health with Wage Stream, our smart platform offering real-time access to wages and tools for effective money management.
